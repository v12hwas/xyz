About Us.

Why RPS ?

Royal Public School, the co – educational English Medium School was established in 2014 by Shree Education Society, out of the deep-rooted concern over the general lack of access to quality education.The institute takes guidance from NCERT curricula and is recognised by Government of Karnataka.

  • RPS aims at providing high quality education to meet the needs of children coming from varied social, cultural and economic backgrounds.

  • Our vision of education through affection and care has proved to be effective and successful and is accepted and appreciated by the community.Prime importance is given to safety and hygiene along with the progressive learning and growth of a child.

  • The committed and dedicated staff members are affectionate, warm and caring too. “Let us live and let others to lead a better life through Education”.

Word from Management

Education in its broadest sense refers to the ways in which one learns to understand the world by gaining knowledge and developing one's skills.Education is a systematic process that includes a variety of learning experiences where the teachers and school play prominent roles. Our objective is to provide each and every child with a clean, safe, comfortable and loving environment to facilitate the learning process in its fullness. Individually and collectively, let us work together to serve the cause of education, for the betterment of the coming generation. Let them live in a world where “ Knowledge is free and the head is held high.”

Faculty

RPS boasts an exceptional faculty dedicated to nurturing the next generation of bright minds. Our team of teachers comprises accomplished professionals with a wealth of experience in their respective fields. They are passionate about teaching and mentorship, committed to providing an enriching educational experience. Their unwavering commitment to fostering academic excellence and personal growth creates an environment where students can thrive. In an era where education is evolving, our faculties are pioneers in adopting innovative teaching methods. Whether it's integrating technology into the classroom or employing experiential learning techniques, they ensure that students remain engaged and grasp concepts in a manner that's both fun and effective.
